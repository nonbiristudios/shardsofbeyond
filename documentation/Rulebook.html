Shards of Beyond

Quick Rules

Introduction

Welcome to Shards of Beyond (SOB), where Realms collide and strategy is key.
Dive into a world where mystical Units from five distinct Realms —

Divine, Elemental, Mortal, Nature and Void
—battle for control over the board.

Will you align with the arcane forces of Nature, the celestial bodies of the Divine, or do you succumb to the abyssal depths of the Void?
Choose your path and outmaneuver your opponent — dominate the battleground and claim victory!

The Objective

The goal is simple: At the end of any Turn, be the first Player to control four Lanes on the 4 (vertical) x 5 (horizontal) Game Board.
There are a total of 9 Lanes; 4 vertical and 5 horizontal ones.

You control a Lane, whether horizontal or vertical, if

  • that Lane contains no more empty Slots,
  • and if your Units in that Lane have a higher combined Power than your Opponent's Units.

Setup

Starting out, take all cards and place all Terrain Cards in a pile. Shuffle it.
Next to each Horizontal Lane on the Game Board, place a random Terrain Card.

Grab your Deck -

The Battle may start.

Turn Sequence

Each Player shuffles their Deck and draws their initial Hand of 6 cards. You may shuffle any number of cards from their hand into their deck and draw that many new ones.
Decide who takes their first Turn; The starting player does not draw a card on their first Turn.

At the start of your Turn, you draw a card. If you started the game, don't draw a Card on your first turn!
During your Turn, you may do any of the following actions:

  1. Crystallize: Transform a card from your Hand into a Crystal by placing it into your Crystal Zone in front of you.
  2. Play: You may play a Unit by deploying it into a Slot on the Game Board. When you deploy a Card, you move it into that Slot.
    • You can play Units either normally (face-up), if you meet their Requirements. c.f. "Card Structure"
    • You can also play Units buried (face-down), even if you don't meet their requirements! Buried Units have no Power, but some Units can ambush, or it could simply be played as a bluff!
    • You can deploy Units in Slots which are not yet occupied by another Unit.

Card Structure

Name
The name of this card.
Power
Higher Power makes it easier to conquer and control Lanes.
Types
Cards can interact with certain types.
A card can have up to two different Types!
Realms
The Realm(s) this card belongs to.
When this is played as a Crystal, the Realm of this Card can help you play more cards of the same Realms.
A card can have up to two different Realms!
Text
The special, individual text of this card.
Each Card requires you to control a certain number of Crystals with certain Realms. If you control Crystals that match each of the Requirements, you can play that card!
Crystals
Indicates, which Crystals of which Realm you need to control in order to play this card.
