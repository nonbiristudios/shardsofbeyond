Shards of Beyond

Quick Rules

Introduction

Welcome to Shards of Beyond (SOB), where realms collide and strategy is key.
Dive into a world where mystical Units from five distinct Realms —

Divine, Elemental, Mortal, Nature and Void
—battle for control over the board.

Will you align with the arcane forces of Nature, the celestial bodies of the Divine, or do you succumb to the abyssal depths of the Void?
Choose your path and outmaneuver your opponent — dominate the battleground and claim victory!

The Objective

The goal is simple: At the end of any Turn, be the first Player to control four Lanes on the 4 x 5 Game Board.

You control a Lane, whether horizontal or vertical, if

  • that Lane contains no more empty Slots,
  • and if your Units in that Lane have a higher combined Power than your Opponent's Units.

Setup

Starting out, take all cards and sort out all Terrain into a separate pile.
Sort all other cards by their Rarity and create a pile for each Common, Uncommon and Rare card. Shuffle each of the piles and put them face-down.

Each Player creates their initial Deck by drawing the following random cards:

  • 3 Rare cards.
  • 9 Uncommon cards.
  • 18 Common cards.
The Battle may start.

Turn Sequence

Each Player shuffles their Deck and draws their initial Hand of 6 cards. You may shuffle any number of cards from their hand into their deck and draw that many new ones.
Decide who takes their first Turn; The starting player does not draw a card on their first Turn.

At the start of your Turn, you draw a card. If you started the game, don't draw a Card on your first turn!
During your Turn, you may do any of the following actions:

  1. Crystallize: Transform a card from your Hand into a Crystal by placing it into your Crystal Zone in front of you.
  2. Play: You may play a Unit by deploying it into a Slot on the Game Board.
    • You can deploy Units either normally (face-up), or buried (face-down). Buried Units have no Power, but can either ambush or be a bluff!
    • You can only play a Unit if you meet its Requirements by controlling fitting Crystals!
    • You can deploy Units in Slots which are not yet occupied by another Unit.
